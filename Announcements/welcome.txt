**Welcome to CryptoStykz!**
As a welcome gift, you'll start off with 5,000 points in your balance to bet with, and all betting takes place in the "fighting" channel.
I recommend you read through the "commands" channel to get a feel for what's available to you, and the "faq" channel to get and idea of how things work.
You can also type ``!help`` for a list of commands, or ``!help bet`` for information on the ``!bet`` command.
